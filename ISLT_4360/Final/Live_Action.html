Knife to the Face
This is a clip of my 1610 Graphic Design instructor, Chip Gubera's, movie trailer which I compiled. My professor is a midwest native and wanted to release a movie about the tradgey the people of Joplin went through in 2011. So for an early assignment, Proffesor Gubera assigned the class in putting together the video, audio, and music to make a trailer which had a clear mood of tradgedy. I think I did a good job but could have dedicated a little more time in the music. I learned that working within the contraints of copyrights was a tough and tedious process. I always wondered how to make cool, tecturized backgrounds and silhouettes in my videos. My first animating mentor, Joseph Erb, taught me a few tricks about blend modes but in my 3640 Graphics designs I learned how to blend compositions and utilize different modes to capture any look I wanted.
