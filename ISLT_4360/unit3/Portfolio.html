My About Page

Hello, all! My name is Carlos Romious and I am coming close to graduating the University of Missouri with a degree in digital storytelling. I started college looking for an engineering degree, following in the footsteps of my mom, but decided to pursue a more creative route. After researching the field, consulting with a few trusted mentors, and a week of self-reflection I decided to take my first intro to digital storytelling class in fall semester of 2016.

“What’s more interesting than creativity? Nothing. And I know you don’t understand now, but I hope you remember this moment when you do.” Ron Benning

Since the fall of 2015 I have been

  • camera-manning on scenes
  • recording and mixing audio clips
  • designing posters
  • cutting trailers
  • lighting sets
  • voice acting
  • and (my favorite) ANIMATING!!

I have loved animating since the first walk cycle I produced. My professor was so passionate and inspired me to animate whatever comes to mind.

“Honey, let me tell you something: I’ve struggled long and hard to get where I am. Take a look at my face. You don’t win people on beauty with this face and the only reason I have gotten to where I am is everything I put on the air I believe in a hundred percent. I’ll bust my balls, do something, and throw it out if it sucks because I will not rip my audience off.” Howard Stern

About a half a year ago I won two animation competitions and now work for my school to promote sports and fun campus activities. Brand images, motion graphics, posters, editing. I have done it all. Let me know if you’d be interested in me doing it for you too.
